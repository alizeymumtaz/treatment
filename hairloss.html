Home Remedies For hair Loss

Causes:
Stress,hormonal imbalance,poor nutrition,and excessive styling.

  • 1. Coconut Oil Massage Why it works: Coconut oil penetrates the hair shaft, reduces protein loss, and moisturizes the scalp. How to use: Warm the oil slightly and massage it into your scalp for 5–10 minutes. Leave it on for at least an hour or overnight before washing..
  • 2. Onion Juice Why it works: Rich in sulfur, which helps regenerate hair follicles and improve circulation. How to use: Blend an onion, extract the juice, apply to the scalp, and leave for 15–30 minutes. Wash thoroughly with a mild shampoo.
  • 3. Aloe Vera Why it works: Soothes the scalp, conditions hair, and reduces dandruff—all contributing to reduced hair loss. How to use: Apply fresh aloe vera gel directly to your scalp and leave for 30–60 minutes before rinsing..
  • 4. Fenugreek Seeds Why it works: High in protein and nicotinic acid, which strengthen hair and reduce shedding. How to use: Soak seeds overnight, grind into a paste, and apply to the scalp for 30–45 minutes. Rinse with lukewarm water.
  • 5. Rosemary Oil Why it works: Known to stimulate hair growth by improving cellular generation. How to use: Mix a few drops with a carrier oil (like coconut or jojoba) and massage into your scalp 2–3 times a week.
